This is the design for a Pixel 4 watch application that will help me keeping track of where I am while running and walking a 5K race.
I am not a great runner, so I will walk and based on my health I will run.
I want the watch to help me:
First the settings I can enter and save:
- length of the race (on Miles or meters or km) (default 2 miles)
- my goal: in minutes, seconds (default for 2 miles 30 mins)
- time to walk: default 3 mins. This is the time I expect to walk
- time to run: default 1 mins. This is the time I expect to run
I will interate my race with a serie of walk run walk run sequences until I reach the goal

We need to save this settings with a race name
We need a START and PAUSE and CANCEL actions
When doing START, the app will track the time, track my GPS position for distance, track my steps, track my heart rate
Every time s sequence is starting I want a notice on the watch or even better on my plugged earphones, sayin WALK for n minutes or Run for x minutes.
I want to see how much I am on a given sequence
Bonus: enter a desire heart reate when walking and when running and alarm when too high
I want the watch to tell me If I am on track, or to slow or too fast (Which would be a nice encouragment)

After the race is done (miles ok km) stop and capture all data that could be shown based on a race name or id\\
